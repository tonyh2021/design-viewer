Touch Targets

Pill buttons visually expanded to meet 44px WCAG AAA on mobile. Frap at 56px + 0.8rem tap offset clears minimum comfortably.

Collapsing Strategy

  • Nav: 64 → 72 → 83 → 99px · hamburger below tablet
  • Hero: 40/60 split → stacked mobile
  • Gift grid: 5 → 4 → 3 → 2 → 1
  • Gutter: 16 → 24 → 40px
  • Status panels: 3-up → single column mobile
