Design System Inspiration of BMW M

A motorsport-engineering interface anchored on a near-black canvas with white BMW Type Next Latin display headlines in confident UPPERCASE. Brand voltage comes from full-bleed automotive photography.

01 — Color Palette

Black canvas, white type, M tricolor accent

The system has no decorative voltage — energy comes from full-bleed photography. The M tricolor stripe is the only true brand-identity color signal; it never appears as a button fill.

Touch Targets

  • Primary CTA at min 48 × 48px — meets WCAG AAA.
  • Icon button at exactly 48 × 48.
  • Text input height 48px.

Collapsing Strategy

  • Top nav collapses to hamburger; menu opens as full-screen black overlay with M-stripe at top.
  • Photography stays full-bleed at every breakpoint.
  • Card grids reduce columns rather than scaling cards down.
  • Spec tables collapse 4→2→1; values stay at display-sm.
  • M-stripe stays 4px height across breakpoints.
